Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

TheoryPass is operated by Sixth Element. We help English-speaking expats in Spain prepare for the DGT theory driving exam. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at support@theorypass.app.

What data we collect

  • Account data: your email address and name, provided when you create an account.
  • Usage data: your answers to practice questions, accuracy per topic, streak, exam date, and pass probability score.
  • Purchase data: if you subscribe to TheoryPass Pro, payment is processed by Apple. We do not store your payment details.

How we use your data

  • To provide the app and personalise your study experience.
  • To calculate your pass probability and track your progress.
  • To send you account-related communications (e.g. password reset).
  • We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted in the EU. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by emailing support@theorypass.app.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Apple App Store — payment processing
  • Anthropic — AI-powered explanations (no personal data is sent)

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, email us at support@theorypass.app. We will respond within 30 days.

Children

TheoryPass is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email.
